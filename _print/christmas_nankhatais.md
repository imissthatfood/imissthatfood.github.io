# Ingredients

* 400 grams butter/ghee
* 400 grams sugar
* 800 grams flour



# Method

Beat ghee well to remove all water.

Knead all ingredients together really well.

Keep overnight or at least 4 hours.

Make small balls without cracks and bake.
