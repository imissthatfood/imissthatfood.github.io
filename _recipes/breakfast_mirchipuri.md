# Ingredients

* 1 pav wheat flour
* 4 green chillies
* salt to taste
* hing powder for flavor




</div> <div class='column is-two-thirds p-3' markdown='1'>

# Method

Crush green chillies; add water, salt and hing powder.

Add flour and knead well. (Puri Dough Consistency)

Make balls, roll and fry puris.
