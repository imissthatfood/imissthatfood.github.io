# Ingredients

* 1/2 kilo chicken
* 1 bowl sour curd
* 1 bowl of milk
* 3 tomatoes
* 1 big onion ground
* 1 teaspoon chilli powder
* finely cut green chillies
* ginger garlic paste
* garam masala powder
* ghee


# Method


Mix all ingredients together and cook on a slow fire till tender.

Sprinkle a little water if necessary.

Garnish with Kasuri methi, coriander and pudina.
