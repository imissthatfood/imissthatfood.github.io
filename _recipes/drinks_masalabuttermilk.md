# Ingredients

* green chillies
* ginger
* salt
* butter milk
* juice of lime
* oil
* mustard seeds
* curry leaves



</div> <div class='column is-two-thirds p-3' markdown='1'>

# Method

Crush green chillies, ginger and salt very fine.

Add to the butter-milk, which should be very watery.

Add juice of a lime.

Season with oil, mustard seeds, and curry patta.
