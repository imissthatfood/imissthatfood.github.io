# Ingredients

* 850 grams mango pulp 
* 1 1/2 kg sugar
* 2 teaspoons citric acid
* 1 pinch potassium metabisulphate 
* 850 ml water 




</div> <div class='column is-two-thirds p-3' markdown='1'>

# Method

Make syrup cool and add citric acid.

Then add the pulp.
 
Lastly add potassium metabisulphate.
