# Ingredients


* 1 cup urad dal
* 2 cups rice
* Salt to taste
* Oil for frying



</div> <div class='column is-two-thirds p-3' markdown='1'>

# Method

Grind together washed and soaked rice and dal till done.

Add salt.

Ferment the batter for ten hours.

Make slightly thick dosas.
