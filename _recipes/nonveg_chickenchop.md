# Ingredients

* onions
* ginger
* garlic
* green chillies
* coriander leaves
* vinegar
* salt
* pepper.


</div> <div class='column is-two-thirds p-3' markdown='1'>

# Method

Grind all together to a smooth paste.

Marinate chicken in this mixture for two days.

Coat in beaten egg.

Coat in bread crumbs.

Chill.

Shallow fry.
