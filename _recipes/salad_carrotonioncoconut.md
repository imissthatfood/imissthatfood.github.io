# Ingredients

* Finely grated carrot
* Finely cut onion
* Two tbsp. coconut gratings
* Salt to taste
* Coriander leaves for garnishing
* 1 small green chili
* juice of one lime
This salad can be made omitting the onion, and keeping the rest of the ingredients the same.
