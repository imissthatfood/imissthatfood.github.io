# Ingredients

* 1/2 pav thuvar dhal
* 2 slit chillies
* salt
* oil (preferably ghee)
* mustard
* red chillies
* curry leaves
* hing


</div> <div class='column is-two-thirds p-3' markdown='1'>

# Method

Boil thuvar dhal.

Churn.

Bring to required consistency with water.

Add 2 slit chillies and salt.

Bring to the boil.

Season with ghee, mustard seeds, 1 broken red chilli, hing and curry leaves.

Cover.
